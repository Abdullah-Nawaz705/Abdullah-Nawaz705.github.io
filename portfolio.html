Accessibility Consultant Collaboration — University of Glasgow

I contributed as an accessibility consultant for the University of Glasgow, focusing on enhancing digital accessibility for blind users. My work centered on testing and improving the compatibility of accessibility software with MathJax and LaTeX-to-HTML conversions, ensuring that complex mathematical content is rendered clearly in UEB braille for HumanWare devices such as the Braille Note Touch Plus.

This project involved in-depth accessibility testing, feedback, and collaboration with software developers to refine accessibility tools that bridge the gap between technical academic content and screen reader/braille device compatibility. My role aimed to promote inclusive education by enabling seamless access to mathematical content for blind students and researchers.

The collaboration also opened avenues for future partnerships, including potential contributions to open-source accessibility projects and ongoing development of accessible content for higher education environments.
