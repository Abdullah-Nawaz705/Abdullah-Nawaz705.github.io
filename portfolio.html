Portfolio & Professional Experience

This portfolio demonstrates my deep commitment to digital accessibility and the unique expertise I bring to every project. My work focuses on creating genuinely inclusive digital experiences that go beyond mere compliance.

Accessibility Consultant — University of Glasgow Collaboration

As an accessibility consultant for the University of Glasgow, I focused on enhancing digital access for blind users. My work involved rigorous testing to improve the compatibility of accessibility software with MathJax and LaTeX-to-HTML conversions, ensuring complex mathematical content renders clearly in Unified English Braille (UEB) for HumanWare devices like the BrailleNote Touch Plus.

This project required deep collaboration with developers to refine tools that bridge the gap between technical academic content and assistive technology. My role helped promote inclusive education by enabling seamless access to STEM materials for blind students and researchers.
